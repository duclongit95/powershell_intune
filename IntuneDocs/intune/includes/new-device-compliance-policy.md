1. Sign in to the [Azure portal](https://portal.azure.com).
2. Select **All services**, filter on **Intune**, and select **Microsoft Intune**.
3. Select **Device compliance** > **Policies** > **Create Policy**.
4. Enter a **Name** and **Description**.
